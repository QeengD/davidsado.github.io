Netflix Filmography Exploration: Unveiling Trends and Insights in Movie Durations

This data analysis project delves into the world of Netflix filmography, utilizing Python's pandas and matplotlib libraries. Beginning with our friend's provided data, the project involves creating a dictionary and subsequently a DataFrame to examine trends in average movie durations from 2011 to 2020. Further exploration includes loading external Netflix data, subsetting, and visualizing insights through scatter plots and categorical color coding based on genre. The project concludes with an analysis addressing the question of whether movies are getting shorter, offering a comprehensive exploration of movie trends on the popular streaming platform.
